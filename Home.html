Core Features

GPS-Based Attendance

Mark your attendance with location verification. Check-in and check-out with automatic working hours calculation and attendance history tracking.

Quick Leave Application

Apply for leaves in seconds with our intuitive form. Choose from casual, sick, emergency, or personal leave types.

Real-time Balance Tracking

Monitor your leave balance in real-time. See total, used, and available leaves at a glance with visual progress bars.

Manager Approvals

Managers can review and approve team leave requests with comments. Streamlined approval workflow for efficiency.

Leave History & Salary Reports

Access complete leave history and detailed salary slips. Track all your past leaves and monthly earnings in one place.

Half-Day Leave Support

Apply for half-day leaves (morning or afternoon) for single dates. Full flexibility for your leave planning.

Birthday Wishes & Special Events

Personalized birthday greetings and updates on special company events. Celebrate moments and stay connected.

Holiday Calendar Integration

Automatic exclusion of weekends and company holidays from leave calculations. Smart business day counting.

Attendance Mispunch

Correct attendance discrepancies seamlessly. Submit mispunch requests for missed punches or technical issues with ease.

Shift Management

Stay on top of your schedule. View assigned shifts, weekly rosters, and timing changes in real-time.

Employee Documents

Secure digital repository. Access and manage your essential employment documents, ID proofs, and letters anytime.
